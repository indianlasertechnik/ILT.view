About INDIAN LASER TECHNIK (I L T)

Established in 2018, Indian Laser Technik (ILT) specializes in the manufacturing of the sheet and tubes Fiber Laser Cutting Machines.

The company also provides CNC Press Brake Machines and a wide range of laser consumables.

Under the leadership of Mr. Mahesh Bhimrao Katkar, ILT has built a strong reputation for delivering high-precision machines, reliable solutions, and excellent service while maintaining strong ethical business practices.
